Unlike most other NFT collections in the cryptospace, we believed that the artwork generation must be community driven with user input, whenever applicable. With this in mind, we started our Discord, Twitter, and Instagram accounts even when our project was still in the drawing board.

As a means of recognizing our avid supporters and participants, we wanted to generate our most rarest artworks using the themes provided by users. With this in mind, we accepted theme requests from Discord users.

The three artworks displayed above are examples of user-driven themes.
